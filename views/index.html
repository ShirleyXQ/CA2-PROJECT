Temple of Heaven

The Temple of Heaven was the place where the emperor worshiped the emperor and prayed for the harvest of grain during the Ming and Qing dynasties. The Temple of Heaven is the general name for the two altars of Luqiu and Qiqiu. The altar has two walls, forming an altar inside and outside. The walls of the altar are in the north and south, symbolizing heaven and earth.

Hangzhou West Lake

West Lake, located in the west of Hangzhou City, Zhejiang Province, is one of the first national key scenic spots and one of China's ten scenic spots. It is one of the major ornamental freshwater lakes in mainland China, and it is also one of the few in the World Heritage List and the only lake cultural heritage in China.

The Great Wall

The Great Wall, also known as the Great Wall of China, is an ancient Chinese military defensive project. It is a tall, sturdy, continuous and long-lasting moat that restricts the enemy from riding. The Great Wall is not a merely isolated wall, but a defense system that combines urban walls, barriers, kiosks and landmarks with the city walls as the main body.The Great Wall is a tourist attraction.

Popular Places

China's coastal cities refer to cities on the Chinese coastline. The city on the coastline of China, because of its proximity to the sea, is generally beautiful and has a pleasant climate. Including Sanya, Qingdao, Rizhao, Weihai, Xiamen, Yantai and many other cities.

  • Sanya

    Sanya City is located at the southernmost tip of Hainan Island, where the planned urban area is 37 square kilometers. It is 91.6 kilometers long from east to west and 51 kilometers wide from north to south. The entire area is surrounded by mountains in the north and the sea in the south. The terrain is gradually inclined from north to south, forming a narrow and long polygonal shape. The coastline of the country is 209.1 kilometers long and there are 19 large and small harbors.

  • Haikou

    The scenery is beautiful, there are many places of interest and historical sites. In addition, many tourist areas and attractions have been newly developed in recent years. The content of the tour is very rich. More famous are Wanlv Park, Holiday Beach, Tropical Ocean World, Binhai Park, etc. Each area has its own special features. It is a multi-functional, integrated national tropical environment integrating sightseeing, vacation, recreation, fitness, business exhibition, and culture and entertainment. Coastal tourism and leisure resort!

  • Hailing Island

    Yangling Hailing Island is located on Hailing Island in Yangjiang and enjoys the reputation of “Southern Beidaihe” and “Oriental Hawaii”. It is known as an uncarved emerald.Hailing Island is part of the Hailing Island Economic Development Experimental Area in Jiangcheng District of Yangjiang City. It is the fourth largest island in Guangdong and is located on the southwest coast of Yangjiang City.
